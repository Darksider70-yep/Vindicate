# Metric Design Framework

## Core Health Metrics

- Active institutions (30d unique issuing institutions)
- Credential issuance rate (daily and monthly)
- Verification volume (daily and monthly)
- Revocation integrity rate (valid revocations / total revocations)
- Network growth velocity (MoM change across institutions + verifiers)
- Token velocity (on-chain settlement turnover / circulating supply)

## Quality and Security Metrics

- Fraud-confirmed issuance ratio
- Slashing case approval ratio
- False-report ratio in slashing court
- Average stake coverage per approved issuer
- Governance participation rate (voting power participation)

## Healthy Protocol Indicators

- Verification growth tracks or exceeds issuance growth
- Stable or improving revocation integrity rate
- Increasing active institutions with low fraud ratios
- Moderate token velocity with strong utility transaction share
- High governance participation and diversified voter base

## Bubble Indicators

- Token price growth decoupled from verification and issuance growth
- Sharp token velocity spikes without utility growth
- High governance concentration in few wallets
- Rapid issuance growth with deteriorating integrity metrics
- Grants outflow with weak integration retention

## Suggested Dashboard Cadence

- Weekly: growth and security snapshots
- Monthly: treasury runway and token utility review
- Quarterly: governance decentralization and incentive recalibration
