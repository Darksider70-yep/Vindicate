# 3-Year Global Rollout Roadmap

## Pilot Country Selection Logic

Countries are ranked with a weighted readiness score:

- Regulatory clarity for digital credentials and data protection (25%).
- Digital identity and trust infrastructure maturity (20%).
- Higher-education digitization and registrar readiness (20%).
- Employer demand for verifiable credentials (20%).
- Partner ecosystem availability (universities, HR platforms, implementation partners) (15%).

## Launch Waves

- Wave 1 (high readiness): jurisdictions with clear privacy frameworks, active digital identity programs, and strong institutional partners.
- Wave 2 (expansion): jurisdictions with high employer demand but moderate policy fragmentation.
- Wave 3 (scale): broader rollout with localized compliance packs and regional partner channels.

## Public Governance Transition Plan

1. Charter phase: publish role boundaries, emergency powers, and sunset rules for centralized controls.
2. Delegation phase: move policy classes (non-critical) to DAO-approved execution pathways.
3. Shared-control phase: require multi-stakeholder approval for treasury and standards profile changes.
4. Majority-decentralized phase: remove non-emergency unilateral controls, retain audited emergency circuit-breakers.

## Decentralization Milestones (Quantified)

- At least 40% of effective voting power outside founding entities by end of Year 2.
- At least 60% of governance proposals initiated by non-core participants by end of Year 3.
- 100% publication of governance rationale and voting analytics for all protocol-impacting proposals.
- Independent governance audits performed annually starting Year 2.
