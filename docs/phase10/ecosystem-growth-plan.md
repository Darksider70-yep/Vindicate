# Ecosystem Growth Plan

## Developer Documentation Strategy

- Maintain one canonical docs portal with:
  - quickstart paths by persona (issuer, verifier, platform integrator, government partner)
  - standards profile references and compatibility matrices
  - reference architectures for campus, platform, and enterprise deployments
- Publish versioned migration guides for every protocol/API major change.
- Ship executable integration templates aligned with OpenAPI and SDK packages.

## Open Research Collaboration Program

- Establish an academic advisory council for credential integrity, DID interoperability, and privacy engineering.
- Co-author public research papers and benchmark reports with partner universities.
- Maintain an open dataset of anonymized protocol performance and integrity metrics for independent analysis.

## Academic Partnership Grants

- Grant tracks:
  - campus integration acceleration
  - interoperability research
  - credential quality and fraud detection studies
- Funding releases are milestone-based with publishable outputs.
- Grant governance uses transparent scoring and conflict-of-interest disclosures.

## Hackathons and Builder Campaigns

- Two global hackathon cycles per year with region-specific qualifiers.
- Challenge tracks: verification tooling, compliance automation, wallet UX, and employer workflow integration.
- Post-hackathon continuation grants for high-retention projects.

## Issuer Certification Program

- Multi-level certification:
  - Level 1: standards-compliant issuer onboarding
  - Level 2: operational security and incident readiness
  - Level 3: high-assurance issuer with advanced governance participation
- Annual recertification includes policy and security control review.

## Ecosystem KPI Targets

- Integration lead time reduction quarter-over-quarter.
- Growth in active issuer and verifier developer accounts.
- Certification completion rate for institutions and issuers.
- Share of credentials issued under standards-aligned profile bundles.
- Employer-side repeat verification rate.
