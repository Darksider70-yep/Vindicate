# Phase 10: Global Adoption Strategy and Standards Diplomacy

Vindicate Phase 10 defines the operating model to move from a technically strong protocol to a globally recognized credential trust standard.

## Strategic Objective

Establish Vindicate as a neutral verification rail that can operate in:

- Web3-native ecosystems that prioritize open participation and composability.
- Regulated institutional ecosystems that require legal accountability, procurement-grade support, and compliance evidence.

## Execution Principles

- Standards-first data model: all new credential types must map to open standards before custom extensions.
- Regulation-aware architecture: no personal data on-chain, verifiable proofs off-chain, revocation transparency on-chain.
- Partner-specific distribution: segment integrations by partner type and procurement maturity.
- Measurable trust: publish public trust metrics, audit summaries, and incident response outcomes.
- Progressive decentralization: move from stewarded governance to broad stakeholder governance with explicit milestones.
