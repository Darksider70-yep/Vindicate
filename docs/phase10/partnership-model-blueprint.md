# Partnership Model Blueprint

This blueprint defines how Vindicate converts standards alignment into durable institutional distribution.

## Partner Segment Matrix

| Partner Segment | Value Proposition | Integration Model | Incentive Alignment | Risk Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Universities and colleges | Tamper-evident diplomas and transcript credentials; reduced fraud; faster verification acceptance | SIS/LMS connector, registrar dashboard, API-based issuance, revocation controls | Lower verification overhead, stronger graduate employability signal, reputation score uplift | Contractual issuer policy, staged rollout by department, dual-control revocation approvals |
| Online learning platforms | Portable micro-credentials and completion proofs across borders | API-first integration with automated issuance webhooks and Open Badges profile support | Higher course completion conversion and partner employer acceptance | Anti-spam issuance thresholds, periodic quality audits, abuse-triggered throttling |
| Certification providers | High-assurance, machine-verifiable certificates and renewal status | Batch issuance service, verification endpoint embedding, certificate lifecycle APIs | New product tier for trusted digital certification and reduced forgery losses | Strict identity proofing policy pack, mandatory stake/bond and slashing exposure |
| Governments and public agencies | Public-sector compatible verification standard with auditability and policy controls | National or state trust-registry bridge, sovereign-hosted gateway option, policy-aware verifier mode | Lower document fraud, interoperable public records, workforce mobility benefits | Jurisdiction-specific legal wrapper, procurement controls, data residency options |
| Hiring platforms and ATS providers | Faster and cheaper candidate credential checks with confidence scores | Verification API, plug-in widgets, webhook events for revocation updates | Lower screening costs and lower false-positive hiring risk | SLA-backed uptime commitments, fallback verification channels, transparent incident reporting |
| HR SaaS and enterprise HRIS providers | Embedded credential verification as a differentiated compliance and trust feature | White-label UI components + API package + enterprise IAM integration | New premium product line and measurable enterprise retention uplift | Tenant isolation controls, compliance evidence packs, contractual DPA and audit terms |

## Partnership Lifecycle

1. Design partner stage: 2-4 anchor institutions per segment to co-define implementation profiles.
2. Lighthouse stage: public reference deployments with measurable outcomes and case studies.
3. Scale stage: partner program with repeatable integration playbooks and regional channel partners.

## Commercial and Governance Model

- Standard partnership agreement with technical addendum and security appendix.
- Shared KPI framework: issuance quality, verification latency, revocation response, and adoption retention.
- Governance participation rights increase as partners meet quality and transparency thresholds.
- Dispute pathway: operational dispute runbook first, then governance arbitration if unresolved.

## Diplomatic Operating Principles

- Maintain protocol neutrality: no exclusive issuance privileges for a single institution type.
- Support sovereign deployment preferences without fragmenting credential semantics.
- Keep standards profiles open and versioned, so partners can build without lock-in risk.
